Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is an aromatic, mouth-watering, and rich one-pot meal that has become a staple of South Asian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • basmati rice
  • chicken
  • yogurt
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • chili powder
  • turmeric
  • garam masala
  • bay leaves
  • cardamom
  • cloves

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken with marinade ingredients.
  2. Prepare Onions: Fry sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Cook Chicken: Add spices and marinated chicken to the pot and cook.
  4. Par-Boil Rice: Partially cook basmati rice.
  5. Layer: Layer the par-boiled rice over the cooked chicken, adding fried onions and herbs.
  6. Dum Cook: Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat.
  7. Serve: Let the biryani rest before serving.
