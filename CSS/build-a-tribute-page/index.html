Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Biography

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.
  • 1954: Graduated in Physics from the University of Madras.
  • 1960: Joined DRDO and later ISRO as an aerospace engineer.
  • 1980: Key role in India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • 1998: Led the Pokhran-II nuclear test mission.
  • 2002–2007: Served as the 11th President of India.
  • 2015: Passed away doing what he loved most — teaching.

Dr. Kalam was admired for his humility, discipline, and belief in empowering youth. He remains an eternal inspiration to millions around the globe.
